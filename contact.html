Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for admission?

Visit our Admissions page for detailed information about the application process.

When can I visit the school?

Campus tours are available Monday-Friday. Please contact the Admissions Office at admissions@ecwagoodnews.edu to schedule a visit.

What is the school calendar?

The academic year runs from September to June with a break in December/January. Details are on our website.

Are scholarships available?

Yes! We offer academic, sports, and arts scholarships. See our Admissions page for details.

How can I contact my child's teacher?

Parents can request teacher contact information through the Student Affairs office (Ext. 102).

Is transportation provided?

We offer school buses for students. Inquire at the main office for route information and fees.
