Admissions

We welcome applications from talented and motivated students who are seeking a high-quality education in a supportive and innovative environment. Here you'll find all the information you need about our admissions process.

Admission Requirements

General Requirements:

  • Valid birth certificate or proof of age
  • Previous academic records and transcripts
  • Certificate of good conduct
  • Medical examination report
  • Passport photographs (4 copies)
  • Parents'/Guardians' identification documents
  • Proof of residence

Admission Process

1. Application

Complete the admission form available on our website or at the admissions office. Submit all required documents.

2. Assessment

Take entrance examinations in Mathematics, English, and General Knowledge. Assessment is used for placement.

3. Interview

Participate in a brief interview with our admissions panel to assess motivation and suitability.

4. Decision

Receive admission decision via email or phone. Successful candidates receive an acceptance letter.

5. Registration

Complete the registration process, pay tuition fees, and receive orientation information.

6. Enrollment

Attend orientation and begin your academic journey with ECWA GOODNEWS SCHOOLS GRA.
