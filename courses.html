Academic Programs

ECWA GOODNEWS SCHOOLS GRA offers a comprehensive range of programs designed to meet the diverse needs and interests of our students. Our curricula are carefully crafted to combine academic rigor with practical skills.

🎓 Core Academic Program

Our foundation program covering essential subjects:

  • English Language & Literature
  • Mathematics
  • Sciences (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Studies & History
  • Geography

🔬 Science & Technology

Advanced programs in STEM fields:

  • Advanced Physics
  • Practical Chemistry
  • Computer Science
  • Information Technology
  • Engineering Basics

🎨 Arts & Humanities

Programs fostering creativity and cultural awareness:

  • Visual Arts
  • Music & Performing Arts
  • Literature & Creative Writing
  • Civic Education
  • Philosophy & Ethics

🌍 Foreign Languages

Develop global communication skills:

  • French Language
  • Spanish Language
  • Mandarin Chinese
  • German Language
  • International English

⚽ Sports & Fitness

Comprehensive athletic and wellness programs:

  • Football (Soccer)
  • Basketball
  • Tennis
  • Swimming
  • Physical Fitness

💼 Skills & Entrepreneurship

Practical skills for personal and professional success:

  • Entrepreneurship
  • Digital Marketing
  • Communication Skills
  • Leadership Development
  • Project Management

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of their programs at ECWA GOODNEWS SCHOOLS GRA, students will:

  • ✓ Demonstrate mastery of core academic subjects
