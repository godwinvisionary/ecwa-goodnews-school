About ECWA GOODNEWS SCHOOLS GRA

Our Mission

At ECWA GOODNEWS SCHOOLS GRA, we are dedicated to providing a world-class education that nurtures intellectual, physical, social, and spiritual development. We strive to create an environment where every student can discover their potential and develop the skills necessary for success in the 21st century.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that transforms young minds into innovative thinkers, compassionate leaders, and responsible global citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Values

  • Excellence: We pursue the highest standards in all aspects of education and personal development. ⭐
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our dealings. 🤝
  • Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and create an inclusive community for all. 🌈
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and encourage forward-thinking approaches to learning. 💡
  • Compassion: We foster empathy and social responsibility in our students. ❤️

Our History

ECWA GOODNEWS SCHOOLS GRA was founded with the vision of providing accessible, quality education to all students. Over the years, we have grown into a respected institution known for our academic excellence and holistic approach to student development. Our commitment to nurturing the next generation of leaders remains unwavering.

Leadership Team

Our school is led by experienced educators and administrators who are passionate about making a difference in students' lives. Our leadership team brings together decades of experience in education, management, and student welfare.

Facilities

ECWA GOODNEWS SCHOOLS GRA boasts state-of-the-art facilities designed to support comprehensive learning:

  • 📚 Modern library with over 10,000 volumes and digital resources
  • 🔬 Well-equipped science laboratories for physics, chemistry, and biology
  • 💻 Computer labs with the latest technology and internet connectivity
  • 🎨 Art and music studios for creative expression
  • 🏃 Sports facilities including football field, basketball court, and swimming pool
  • 🍽️ Nutritious meal programs and well-maintained cafeteria

Accreditation & Recognition

ECWA GOODNEWS SCHOOLS GRA is accredited by the National Ministry of Education and recognized by various educational bodies for maintaining high standards of teaching and learning. We are proud recipients of multiple awards for academic excellence and innovative teaching practices.
